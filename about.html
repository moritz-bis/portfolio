I am Moritz Bisjak, a product designer from Germany with a Master (Diplom) from HfG Offenbach.

I believe products are always expressions of bigger systems. Key to my work is therefore a systematic approach: I analyse products in context and strive towards original, functional and aesthetic solutions at the root. With my work, I seek to create social and ecological impact.
